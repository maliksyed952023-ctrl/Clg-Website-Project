Hyperlinking Policy

Policy for External Links

At many places in this website, you will find links to other websites or portals created and maintained by other government, non-government, or private organizations. These links have been placed for your convenience.

When you select a link to an external website, you are subject to the privacy and security policies of the owners or sponsors of that website. Government Polytechnic, Aurangabad does not control or guarantee the accuracy, relevance, or completeness of information on linked websites.

Mere presence of links or listings on this website should not be assumed as endorsement of any kind. The institute does not object to you linking directly to information hosted on this site and no prior permission is required for the same.

However, we do not permit our pages to be loaded into frames on other websites. Our departmental pages must load into a newly opened browser window of the user.
