About Institute

Government Polytechnic Aurangabad is one of the premier technical institutes in Maharashtra established in 1955.

Long-Term Objectives

  • Strengthen collaboration with industries
  • Promote innovation and technology
  • Enhance employability of students

  • Ethical and professional values
  • Continuous improvement
